Sharing animated GIF's with your team mates on Slack has never been more easy. Just add the Giphy Slack Bot to your team and post a GIF of your choice or a random one. You can also see the trending GIF's at this moment.

Get the Giphy Slack bot now.

How to use


/gif shows a random GIF

/gif [category] shows a random GIF for the specified category

/gif -s [search] shows a specific GIF that matches the search term

/gif -t shows the top 10 trending GIF's at the moment

/gif help shows list of  available commands
